FAKE NEWS...?

  • Fake news is news designed to spread hoaxes, propaganda and misinformation deliberately.
  • This is often done to further or impose specific ideas and is usually achieved with political agendas.​​
  • Often, fake news will mimic real headlines and twist the story.

IMPORTANT!

  • ​This is an evolution in human history but, at the same time, unfocused the line between real and maliciously forged media.
  • Today anyone can publish content, credible or not. Sadly,fake news accumulates a great deal of attention over the internet, especially on social media.​​​
